2025 ASH Copper State Cup

Live stream award ceremony Saturday 10/11 from Tempe Kiwanis Park @ 2PM MST depending on the weather. Winners will be posted by Sunday 10/12.

The Arizona Society of Homebrewers (ASH) was founded in 1995 and currently has over 180 active members making it the largest Homebrew Club in Arizona. ASH was recognized as AHA Homebrew Club of the Year in 2019, 2020 & 2025 and is proud to announce the 2025 ASH Copper State Cup.

Registration will open Friday August 1st @ 6:00 AM MST

Registration will close on Sunday, September 14, 2025 6:00 PM, MST or when we reach the entry limit. All entries must be received by our shipping location or at a drop-off location by Monday, September 15, 2025 7:00 PM, MST. Entries will not be accepted beyond this date.

This competition is AHA/BJCP sanctioned and this competition is also a qualifying competition for:

  • ASH Homebrewer of the Year (Limited to ASH members)
  • The Br√º Club Competition Circuit
  • Beehive State Circuit

Entry Limit: 200 (more if we have judges to support this event)

Participants are limited to 10 entries with only 1 entry per sub-category permitted. Submit 2 bottles or cans per entry

Medals will be awarded to 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place in each category/table. ASH HBOY points and multiplier points will be awarded 3 points for 1st place, 2 points for 2nd place, 1 point for 3rd place. Entries for multiplier points may be earned for medaling: 2C - International Dark Lager, 9C - Baltic Porter or M3B - Spice, Herb, or Vegetable Mead.

The 1st place entry in each category will advance to the Best of Show (BOS) round with a single, overall Best of Show beer selected. One BOS trophy will be awarded to the BOS - Beer and one BOS trophy will be awarded to the BOS - Mead / Cider.

Judging will take place from September 19 - October 4 with the awards ceremony taking place at ASH's Fallfest held at Kiwanis Park in Tempe, AZ on Saturday, October 11th. Score sheets will be available Sunday October 12th.

Questions? reach us at competitions@azhomebrewers.org
